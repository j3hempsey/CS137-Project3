About Us

We at Peter Sells Peppers are on a mission: to bring the world's best peppers to your doorstep for you and your family to enjoy. Our founder Peter believes that every man woman and child should never be without great peppers and has been carrying out his mission since 1954.

Peter

 Founder, CEO Jabanero Peter has been around world class peppers his entire life. Growing up with only the best peppers he felt it was his obligation to societuy to bring such treasures to the rest of the world. He founded Peter Sells Peppers in 1954 to make his dream come true and create a world with peppers for all to enjoy.

Justin

 Chief Pepper Engineer Ghost Pepper Justin grew up in a big city. The local grocery stores only sold subpar quality peppers. He felt there was something better out there. It wasn't until his last year at Stanford did he encounter Peter who he was introduced to by a mutual friend. Justin graduated from Stanford Univeristy's prestigious Agricultural Studies program with a specialization in Pepper Sciences. He has been with the company since graduating and is keen on ensuring each of their customers has the best possible pepper experience.

John

 Pepper Experience Engineer Spooky Pepper John once traveled to Chile, the world's central hub of Chilean chillis. When a local showed him what peppers can be, he could never look back. He was encaptured by the idea that peppers could reach such a potential and he needed to share it with the world. John is hell bent on ensuring each of Peter Sells Pepper's customers experiences the magic he felt on that fateful day in Chile.

Jed

 Chief of Pepper QA Purple Jed knows quality. He knows when something is good, bad, and great. More importantly, he knows the difference between a good pepper and a great one. He is certified in 31 countries across 8 continents as a Pepper Master and was trained under Sergeant Pepper, the world renowned Pepper Guru. His expertise has brought Peter Sells Peppers to new heights and he is constantly seeking to improve the quality of peppers in the world.
